help_outline How to Play
1

Guess the Word

Type any valid 5-letter English word and press ENTER to submit your guess.

2

Read the Colors

Each tile changes color after your guess to show how close you were to the secret word.

3

6 Tries to Win

You have up to 6 guesses. Use the clues from each row to narrow down the answer.

Tile Colors

A

Green — correct spot

Right letter, right position

B

Terracotta — wrong spot

In the word, but misplaced

C

Ink — not in word

This letter is not in the word

Example

Guessing CRANE for CHARM:

C
R
A
N
E

C is correct. R, A are in the word but misplaced. N, E are not in the word.

Pro Tips

  • → Start with CRANE or SLATE — they cover the most common letters.
  • → Never reuse ink letters — they're confirmed absent.
  • → Terracotta letters must move to a different position next guess.
  • → Words can have repeated letters, e.g. SPELL or ADDED.

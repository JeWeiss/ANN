help_outline How to Chart
1

Survey a Tile

Left-click any tile to reveal it. The first click is always safe — hazards are placed after it.

2

Read the Markings

Each number tells you how many hazards lie in the 8 surrounding tiles. Use this to deduce safe ground.

3

Place Markers

Right-click (or long-press on mobile) to plant a 🚩 marker on a tile you suspect is dangerous. Mark every hazard to win!

4

Complete the Chart

Reveal all safe ground without hitting a hazard. You win when every safe tile is charted.

Surveyor's Tips

  • → Start from a corner — corners have fewer neighbors, giving you more information early.
  • → If a 1 tile has exactly 1 hidden neighbor, that neighbor is definitely a hazard.
  • → Mark confidently — the hazard counter helps you track what's left.
  • → When stuck, count: if hidden neighbors = the number shown, they're all hazards.
  • → Clear ground cascades automatically — use it to chart large areas fast.

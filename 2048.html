How to Play

Use arrow keys to slide all tiles.

Tiles with the same number merge when they collide.

After each move, a new 2 or 4 appears.

Reach the 2048 tile to win!

Strategy Tips

Keep your highest tile in a corner.

Build a snake pattern from corner outward.

Never move away from your corner if avoidable.

Plan 2–3 moves ahead before sliding.

help_outlineHow to Play
1

Aim your mouse over the board and click to shoot.

2

Match 3 or more same-color bubbles to pop them.

3

Bubbles bounce off walls — use angles to reach tricky spots.

5

Press Space or click NEXT to swap current ↔ next bubble.
